Master Trading Rules

Follow these essential rules to become a successful trader. Discipline is more important than strategy.

Never Risk More Than 0.3-0.5% On A Trade

Protect your capital by limiting risk exposure. Never gamble more than you can afford to lose on any single position.

Never Ever Add To A Losing Position

Averaging down is the fastest way to blow up your account. Cut losses quickly and never throw good money after bad.

Make It Big It Take Times

Real wealth accumulation in trading happens gradually. Be patient, consistent, and understand that sustainable growth requires time.

Add Only On A Winning Position

Pyramid into winning trades, not losing ones. Increase position size only when the market proves you right.

⚠️ The Golden Rule of Trading

Risk/Reward is the mother of becoming a master in trading. Always maintain at least a 1:2 risk-reward ratio. Your potential profit should always be at least twice your potential loss. This single principle separates professional traders from gamblers.
